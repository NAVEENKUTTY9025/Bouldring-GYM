General Questions

Everything you need to get started

Q. What are your opening hours?

We're open 24/7, 365 days a year! Staffed hours are 6am – 11pm, but members with 24/7 access can climb anytime using their key fob.

Q. Do you offer day passes?

Absolutely! Day passes are $25 and include full access to all climbing areas. Gear rental (shoes + harness) is available for an additional $10.

Q. I'm a beginner — where do I start?

We recommend our Intro Pack: $40 for a day pass, gear rental, and a 30-min orientation with a staff member. We'll teach you the ropes (literally!).

Q. Is there parking available?

Yes! We have a free parking lot with 80+ spots. We also have bike racks and we're just 2 blocks from the light rail station.

💳

Membership FAQs

Find the perfect plan for you

Q. How much does a membership cost?

Monthly memberships start at $85/month. Annual memberships are $900 (save $120!). We also offer student and family discounts — ask at the front desk.

Q. Can I freeze my membership?

Yes! You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year at no cost. Just give us 7 days notice and you're all set.

Q. How do I cancel my membership?

We're sad to see you go! You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No hidden fees — just email us at members@summitgrip.com.

Q. Do you offer family discounts?

We sure do! Families of 3+ get 15% off each membership. Kids under 12 climb for half price with a paying adult.

🧗

Climbing & Training

Level up your skills

Q. How often do you change routes?

Our routesetting team resets sections weekly. Bouldering gets refreshed every Monday, roped routes every Thursday. There's always something new!

Q. Can I rent gear at the gym?

Yes! We rent climbing shoes, harnesses, and chalk bags. All gear is professionally cleaned and maintained. Rental packages start at $10.

Q. Do you offer coaching or private lessons?

Absolutely! Our certified coaches offer private 1-on-1 sessions ($65/hr) and group clinics. Check our Training page for upcoming workshops.

Q. Is there a youth climbing team?

Yes! Our SummitGrip Youth Team competes regionally and nationally. Tryouts are held twice a year. Contact our Youth Program Director for details.

🎉

Events & Community

Get involved with the crew

Q. Can I book a private event?

Yes! We host birthday parties, corporate team-building, and private group sessions. Packages start at $350 for up to 10 people. Contact us for a quote.

Q. When are your climbing competitions?

We host 4 major comps per year: Winter Bouldering Classic, Spring Lead Climbing Cup, Summer Speed Series, and Fall Youth Invitational. Check our Events page for dates.

Q. How do I meet other climbers?

We have weekly community meetups every Wednesday at 7pm — all levels welcome! Also, check our community board or join our Slack channel to find climbing partners.

Q. Can I volunteer at SummitGrip?

We love volunteers! Help with comps, youth programs, or community cleanups. Email volunteer@summitgrip.com to get started.
