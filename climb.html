Climbing Etiquette

Safe, respectful, and fun for everyone

🧹 Clean Your Chalk

Brush your chalk off holds when you're done. Keep the walls clean for the next climber.

👀 Look Before You Leap

Always check that the route you're starting is clear. Look up for other climbers before you climb.

💬 Communicate Clearly

Use standard belay commands: "On belay?" "Belay on." "Climbing." "Climb on."
