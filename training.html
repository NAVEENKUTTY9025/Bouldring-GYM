Training Tips From Our Coaches

Level up with expert advice

🔥 Warm Up Properly

Start with 10 minutes of light cardio, dynamic stretching, and easy climbing. Your fingers and shoulders will thank you.

🎯 Focus on Weaknesses

If you're bad at slopers, spend 20% of each session on them. Targeted training yields the fastest gains.

🧘 Rest & Recovery

Climbing is 50% training, 50% recovery. Take rest days seriously — your muscles grow when you rest.
