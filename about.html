Our Story

What started as a dream between two climbing guides in Boulder has grown into the region's premier indoor climbing destination. SummitGrip opened its doors in 2012 with just 20 routes and a handful of passionate climbers.

Today, we boast over 150 routes, a world-class bouldering cave, and a thriving community of thousands. From hosting youth climbing teams to sending our first 5.15a project, we believe climbing transforms lives.

Our mission: make climbing accessible, inspiring, and endlessly fun for everyone who walks through our doors.
