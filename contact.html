Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reach out

What are your opening hours?

We're open 24/7, 365 days a year. Staffed hours are 6am – 11pm, but members can climb anytime with 24/7 access.

Do you offer day passes?

Absolutely! Day passes are $25, and gear rental is available for $10. First time? Ask about our intro pack.

Can I book a private event?

Yes! We host birthday parties, team-building events, and private coaching. Contact us and we'll make it happen.

Is there parking available?

We have a free parking lot with 80+ spots, plus bike racks and a light rail station 2 blocks away.
